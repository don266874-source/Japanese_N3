About Me & This Journey

Hi there! Welcome to the hub. My name is Felix, and just like you, I am an active Japanese language learner currently battling through the JLPT N3 level.

After reviewing hiragana and katakana and clearing the basic N4 hurdles, I quickly realized that N3 is the ultimate make-or-break stage in Japanese learning. This is where textbooks stop holding your hand. Suddenly, you are hit with complex grammar nuances, native-speed news, and a mountain of kanji that look frustratingly similar.

I didn't build this site as a "professor" or an "expert." I built it as a fellow learner's digital war room. Here, I document my real-world learning process, including:

  • The tricky N3 grammar points that always trip me up during mock exams (like ~kiru vs ~owaru).
  • Practical breakdowns of vocabulary and real-world kanji.
  • The exact Anki decks and mnemonic hacks I use to make things stick.

If you are currently studying for the JLPT N3, or just trying to survive the intermediate Japanese valley, you are not alone. Let's study, mistake-correct, and conquer N3 together!
